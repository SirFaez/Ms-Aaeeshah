How We Met

Before we dated, we"ve known each other for a few years since 2017. I first met her in my ugama class in SOAS, we were in class 4 Alif. During that time, we barely talked or got to know each other.

We met again the next year in PJN during my year 7. We were in the same class, 7B. I've started to notice and took a liking for her, I've been observing her from afar, but was too much of a coward to start interacting with her. I took a liking to her cutesy and bubbly personality, which stood out to me. Throughout the year until year 8, all I did was admire her from afar.

In year 9, she mellowed down a bit, but her charm still lingered on me. After she got together with my closest friend, I sort of gave up, I guess I was too late, but perhaps it was for the better.

In year 11, our final year of school in PJN, she just recently broke up in the earlier months. I thought to myself, maybe I could try again, but taking advantage of the situation seemed wrong, so I rejected the idea.
